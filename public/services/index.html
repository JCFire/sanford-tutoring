TROUBLESHOOTING

Are you home educating and just need a little extra help with difficult concepts? I can fill in those gaps for you.

TEST PREPARATION

Is your child about to take the PSAT, SAT or ACT where a high score can mean scholarship money? I can help prepare your child in a way that will maximize his score.

CO-OPERATIVE INSTRUCTION

Are you home educating but don't feel capable of teaching math? I can take responsibility for that subject leaving you with the time and energy to concentrate on others.

PERSONALIZED DIRECTION

Are you spending a lot of money for group classes in which your child must compete for the teacher's attention? Instead, invest your money in one-on-one, personal tutoring where relevant math concepts are learned and retained. You will receive instruction tailored to your child's needs.

RESISTANT PUPILS

Does your child resist your instruction or help with homework? Let me step in and alleviate his frustration. I can make learning interesting.

HIGH SCHOOL ADVISING

Do you need help planning what your child's high school courses should look like? Do you need help making a high school transcript for college application? I can provide academic advising and transcript preparation.
