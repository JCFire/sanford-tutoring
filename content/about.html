Welcome! I'm Sandy,

I am passionate about math. My aim is to provide a safe, friendly, fun environment where the student will not only be successful but also enjoy learning. I commit to offering your child the care and attention he needs to succeed. I am patient, yet not content to let any student do less than their best. The pace will be tailored to your child's individual needs. I enjoy working with kids from both ends of the spectrum - from those who are behind to those who are seeking more of a challenge.

I am a Florida State Certified Teacher of Mathematics.
I have a bachelor's degree in Math Education and a master's degree in Computer Science from Florida Institute of Technology.
I tutor Pre-algebra, Algebra I and II, Geometry, Trigonometry, Pre-calculus, College Algebra, PSAT, SAT, and ACT Preparation.
I home educated my three sons from kindergarten through high school. All three scored well enough on the PSAT and SAT to earn full ride National Merit Scholarships to college.
